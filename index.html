WHO WE ARE

Twiga Vision Tanzania is a non-for-profit, non-governmental and non-political charity that was created to deal with various issues facing Tanzanian communities. Twiga Vision Tanzania's main office is located in the Mianzini area of Arusha Tanzania. The organization was founded in 2012 and was officially registered in 2013. Eversince, it has been operating in two districts of Arusha Urban and in the Arusha DC district.

WHAT WE DO

We provide different kinds of support to women, children and youth in Tanzania, especially, those whose life backgrounds are challenged in one way or another, which puts them into some kind of vulnerability. The main goal is to give them a better life now and in the future. The intervention is mainly on the areas of education, health, unemployment and livelihood.

OUR MISSION

VISION

Our vision is to enable local communities to achieve individual and collective goals with regards to essential life skills such as food, health education and settlements.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide sustainable development to local Tanzanian communities.

GOALS

Promote education, combat poverty, improve health, raise self-awareness and self-esteem.

Our work mainly targets women, youth and children in Tanzania.

In particular the more disadvantaged members of society such as single mothers and those living in extreme poverty.

PROGRAMS

EDUCATIONAL SPONSORSHIP PROGRAM - SCHOOL PROJECT

This is one of the main programs we have, with this program we reach hundreds of children and youth by providing them with education sponsorship. Our main target is children between the age of 5 and 16 years of age. However, in some cases, we sponsor older individuals as well.

FRIEND FAMILY PROGRAM

Family Friend program is a program that aims at rescuing families that are in a situation where their being kicked out of a house, mainly because of failure to rent or other reasons, which puts the family in danger of being on the street.

WOMEN'S EMPOWERMENT PROGRAM

The Women's Empowerment Program, is a program that was specifically designed to support women in multipel ways. The aim is to enable women realize their position in the community and work towards becoming more independent, both financially and socially.

YOUTH EMPOWERMENT PROGRAM

The Youth Empowerment Program is a program that aims at providing necessary life-skills to young people.

VOLUNTEER PROGRAM

Our volunteer program is one of the most important programs in our organisation. Volunteers have always been a big part of our acheivements. Volunteering with us is a great support to us and a centre of knowledge exchange to both, whereby the volunteer also gains some experience. You can come and volunteer with us directly or come through our partner organisations.

PROJECTS

THE KIPEPE WOMEN'S SEWING GROUP

The KIPEPEO Women's sewing group under Twiga Vision Tanzania is in need of sewing equipments and capital to start a sewing business. This project falls under our Livelihood program that aims at empowering families into acquring self-dependence.

TAKE A MALAIKA PROJECT

Malaika means an angel in Kiswahili (language widely spoken in Tanzania, Kenya, Congo etc.). That is what inspired us to name our project . In Arusha / Tanzania, 9 motivated seamstresses are currently engaged in the production of the small Malaikas. This enables the women to secure a monthly income and they have the chance to lead a financially independent life in Tanzanian society.

DAYCARE PROGRAM

This program aims at helping women from low-income families, who have little children that would keep them from going out to work. Due to pre-existing negative cultural practices, the woman is expected to be at home most of the time; look after the children, especially the little ones, and doing all house work.
